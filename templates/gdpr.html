GDPR

At CKumar.in, we are committed to respecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. Our practices adhere to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), ensuring transparency and accountability in how we collect, use, and protect your information.

What is GDPR?

The GDPR is a European Union regulation designed to give individuals greater control over their personal data. It applies to businesses that process the personal data of EU residents, regardless of where the business is based.

How We Comply with GDPR

We implement GDPR-compliant practices to protect your rights and ensure your data is handled responsibly. Here's how:

  1. Data Collection Transparency

    • We collect only the data necessary for providing our services, such as name, email address, and contact details.
    • You are informed about why and how your data is collected.

  2. Lawful Basis for Processing

    • Your data is processed only with your explicit consent, to fulfill a contract, or to comply with legal obligations.

  3. Your Rights Under GDPR

    • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
    • Right to Rectification: If your data is inaccurate, you can request corrections.
    • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your data in certain circumstances.
    • Right to Restrict Processing: You can limit how your data is used.
    • Right to Data Portability: You can receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
    • Right to Object: You can object to your data being used for certain purposes, such as marketing.

  4. Secure Data Storage

    • Your data is stored securely using robust technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access or breaches.

  5. Third-Party Data Sharing

    • We do not share your data with third parties without your consent, except when required for service delivery or legal obligations.

  6. Cookies and Tracking

    • We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

  7. Data Breach Notifications

    • In the unlikely event of a data breach, affected users will be notified promptly as per GDPR guidelines.

How to Exercise Your Rights

If you wish to exercise any of your GDPR rights or have questions about our data practices, you can contact us at:

Email: privacy@ckumar.in

Updates to This Statement

We may update this GDPR statement periodically to reflect changes in regulations or our practices. Please revisit this page for the latest information.
